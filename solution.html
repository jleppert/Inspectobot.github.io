The InspectoBot

We will save lives by making sure that building disasters do not happen again.

Our mission is to leverage drone technology to lower costs, increase frequency and improve efficiency of structural inspections necessary to ensure the safety of all buildings and construction.


Our product is intended to be user-friendly enough to be operated by relatively unskilled drone-operators. This will result in significant cost savings over utilizing skilled structural engineers onsite.
